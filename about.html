About Me

I was born in the beautiful South of France in 1989 and moved to the United States just two years later. Since then, I have been living in Rockville, MD. My love for computers dates back to when I was in middle school and spent most of my free time playing computer games. From the games, I became interested in how computers work and how I could modify the gaming experience. My friends and I would constantly tinker with the scripts to see what changes we could make to the game. Since then, I have always been interested in learning as much as I can about computers.

This passion for computers guided my decision to choose computer science as my field of study. In 2007, I started working at Daly Computers, which gave me a solid understanding of computer hardware and software. In March 2014, I got one step closer to achieving my career goal by obtaining a degree in Computer and Information Science from the University of Maryland University College. Though I no longer find myself playing computer games, as I did when I was a kid, I still enjoy tinkering with computers and keeping myself up to date in this fast changing world of technology.
